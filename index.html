HEALTH AND SAFETY PROTOCOLS:-

1.pre-class Health Screening:

  • Conduct health screenings or questionnaires for participants before each class to check for symptoms of illness.
  • Ensure participants with symptoms or recent exposure to infectious diseases stay home.

2. Sanitization:

  • Regularly clean and disinfect the studio, including floors, mats, props, and high-touch surfaces.
  • Provide hand sanitizers at the entrance and encourage participants to use them before and after class.

3 Class Size and Social Distancing:

  • Limit the number of participants in each class to allow for adequate spacing.
  • Mark spots on the floor to ensure mats are placed at least 6 feet apart.

4. Ventilation:

  • Ensure proper ventilation in the studio by keeping windows open or using air purifiers.
  • Consider holding classes outdoors when possible.

5. Use of pops:

  • Encourage participants to bring their own mats and props.
  • If studio props are used, ensure they are thoroughly cleaned after each use.
